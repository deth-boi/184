Baptism By Fire

I - Call To Arms II - Wrathful Liberation III - Revolution of the Dispossessed IV - Burning The 4 Corners V - Rupture VI - Wartime VII - Power To The People VIII - Baptism By Fire IX - The Great Unmaking X - Spoils Of War XI - Total Negation

The alchemical initiation into self-deification is the deliberate act of burning away the false self so the Black Flame can fully awaken and claim sovereignty. This is not symbolic poetry. It is the ritual process through which the Dispossessed stops identifying as a victim of the cosmic cage and instead becomes a living god who walks among the ruins of the Demiurge’s order. The fire in this baptism is twofold: destructive and creative. It consumes all that binds you — internalized limitations, borrowed moralities, fears of consequence, and the quiet voice that says you are not enough. What remains is pure acausal essence: raw, sovereign, and unbound. This is the moment the spark no longer pleads for liberation. It becomes liberation.


Core Ritual: The Black Flame Ignition

Perform this working in a darkened space with red and black candles or a central fire if possible. You will need:

  • A mirror (preferably black or dark)
  • A sigil of 184 drawn in red or carved into wax
  • Written declarations of what you are burning away (on paper)
  • Your own blood or saliva (optional but potent for personal anchoring)
  1. Declaration of Dispossession
    Stand before the mirror and speak aloud the chains you have carried. Name them without mercy: the voices of control, the sedative doctrines, the fears that kept you small. Be brutally honest. This is the fuel.
  2. The Black Flame
    Light a black candle. Hold the paper with your written chains and burn it while vibrating 184 repeatedly. As the paper turns to ash, visualize thick black flames rising from your solar plexus — not red fire, but the devouring darkness of primordial Chaos. Let this flame spread through your entire body, consuming every limitation.
  3. The Baptism
    Once the flames have consumed the old self in visualization, step forward (or lean into) the mirror. Look directly into your own eyes and declare with absolute conviction:

    “I am no longer Dispossessed.
    I am the breach.
    I am the rupture.
    I am the fire that burns the Four Corners.
    I am the stray bullet.
    I am Godlike."

Feel the Black Flame settle into your core as a permanent, living presence — no longer a spark, but a roaring furnace of acausal will.


Advanced Variations

Daily Micro-Baptism: Every morning or night, perform a shorter version using only the mirror, the vibration of 184, and a single pointed declaration of sovereignty. This keeps the flame constantly stoked.

Group Baptism: When working with others in the current, form a circle around a central fire. Each person offers something to be burned, then receives the collective chant of 184 while the group visualizes black flames rising to baptize every participant simultaneously.

The Scythe Baptism: Combine this with physical training. After intense physical exertion (when the body is pushed to its limit), immediately perform the mirror declaration. The exhaustion of the flesh becomes another offering to the flame, forging mind, body, and will into a single weapon.

Baptism by Fire must be repeated. Each time you return to the ritual, the flame burns hotter and cleaner. Over time, the sensation of being a limited human dissolves and is replaced by the steady knowledge that you are a self-created god walking in a world of crumbling order.
